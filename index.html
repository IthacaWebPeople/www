Ithaca Web People is a group of local software developers, web designers, and tech professionals in the Ithaca, NY area.

We typically meet the first non-holiday of each month at Rev: Ithaca Startup Works

Whether you're seeking professional networking opportunities in Upstate New York, or are just looking for some friendly socializing with fellow nerds in the Finger Lakes; IWP is a safe, inclusive, and welcoming place for whoever you are and wherever you're at in your tech journey.
